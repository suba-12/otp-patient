Lung Tumor Diagnosis

Here's what you need to know about lung tumor diagnosis:

1. Symptoms

Recognize common symptoms such as persistent cough, chest pain, and shortness of breath.

2. Imaging Tests

Undergo imaging tests like X-rays or CT scans to detect abnormalities in the lungs.

3. Biopsy

A biopsy may be necessary to confirm the presence of a tumor and determine its type.

About Us

This portal is dedicated to lung tumor segmentation. It serves both patients and doctors, allowing doctors to view patient CT images and segment them through the website. Patients can also access their prescriptions, appointments, awareness materials, symptoms, and more.

"Our platform, dedicated to lung tumor segmentation, serves as a vital resource for patients and healthcare professionals alike. Patients benefit from access to their medical records, prescriptions, and appointment scheduling, alongside educational materials covering lung tumor awareness and symptoms. For healthcare professionals, particularly radiologists and oncologists, our platform facilitates the review of patient CT images, segmentation tasks, tumor analysis, and collaborative treatment planning. We prioritize patient privacy and data security, ensuring that all medical information is handled with the utmost confidentiality and complies with relevant healthcare regulations."
